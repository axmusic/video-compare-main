Playback Modes

Control when the videos start playing.

1. Auto (Default)

Videos play automatically and loop.

<div class="uevc-slider-container" data-play-mode="auto">...</div>
Loading

2. Start on Interaction

Videos start paused and begin playing when first hovered or touched.

<div class="uevc-slider-container" data-play-mode="interaction">...</div>
Loading

3. Only Play While Interacting (Manual)

Videos only play when the mouse is over the container or while touching it.

<div class="uevc-slider-container" data-play-mode="manual">...</div>

Customizable Slider Handles

Choose from preset handle styles or provide your own custom icon.

1. Rectangular Bar

<div class="uevc-slider-container" data-handle-type="bar">...</div>

2. Horizontal Arrows

<div class="uevc-slider-container" data-handle-type="arrows">...</div>

3. Custom Icon (FontAwesome or SVG)

Place an element with class uevc-handle-icon inside your container.

<div class="uevc-slider-container" data-handle-type="icon">
    <div class="uevc-handle-icon"><i class="fas fa-arrows-alt-h"></i></div>
    ...
</div>
